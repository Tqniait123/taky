Delete Account

Last updated: November 24, 2025

At TaQy, we respect your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

Warning: Deleting your account is a permanent action. All your personal information, order history, and preferences will be permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.

1. How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly from within the mobile application by following these steps:

  • Open the TaQy app on your device.
  • Log in to your account (if not already logged in).
  • Navigate to the Profile section (usually found in the app bar).
  • Tap on Settings or look for the Delete Account option.
  • Review the confirmation warning.
  • Confirm your choice to permanently delete your account.

2. What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request to delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your personal profile information (Name, Email, Phone Number).
  • Your authentication credentials.
  • Your order history and activity logs.
  • Any preferences or settings saved in the app.

3. Contact Us

If you are unable to access the app or encounter any issues while trying to delete your account, please contact our support team for assistance:
